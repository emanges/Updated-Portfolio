Contact Emily Manges Resume
Originally from Santa Fe, New Mexico, Emily attended Skidmore College in Saratoga Springs, NY. At Skidmore, she was the recepient of the Eleanor Linder Winter Endowed Scholarship. Emily has interned at the Whitney Museum, the Tang Museum and SPRING/BREAK Art Fair. She currently works in the Department of Drawings and Prints at MoMA, where she was hired as the youngest employee in a curatorial department at MoMA. At MoMA, she works on the art acquisitions process, managing the payment and title transfer of every work to enter the Drawings and Prints collection. Emily is passionate about the intersection between art and technology. She hopes to start an art tech company one day. She recenently enrolled in Columbia Engeering's Coding Bootcamp.
